Harry Truman is generally ranked in the middle tier of presidents. But is he really so mediocre? He’s gained a reputation for incompetency, but I personally believe he accomplished some great things while in office and should’ve been elected for a third term.

His foreign policy was a precursor of Reagan, Bush, Kennedy, and nearly every other President of that era, after all, he started the Cold War. And really, you people love Kennedy but hate Truman, who was just like Kennedy but wasn't assassinated. The New Frontier was just a new version of Truman's Fair Deal. The Cuban Missile Crisis was just Kennedy's version of everything that Truman must have handled.

I hate when people underestimate Truman. He basically founded Cold War America.
